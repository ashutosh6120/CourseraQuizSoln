Practice quiz test


1]  When a user is _____ an object, that means it will stay positioned at that point, even when users or cameras are in motion.
Ans:: Placing (sometimes this has been referred to as "anchoring")


2]  What can “scaling” mean in the context of AR?
Ans::  It describes the relative size of a given digital object


3]  What is occlusion?
Ans::  When one image or object blocks another


4]  What is immersion?
Ans::  The sense that AR objects belong in the real world


5]  What is context awareness?
Ans::  Tracking of the physical objects in a space and understanding their relationships to each other
